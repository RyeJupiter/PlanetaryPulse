About

Planetary Pulse is an Earth visualization and analytics platform focused on making landscape impact on local climate visible at a glance.

We see climate as an emergent property of landscapes: soils, water, vegetation, and structure determine how energy and moisture move. When land is degraded, heat and volatility rise. When land is regenerated, cooling, hydration, and resilience return.

We also see beauty as a signal of synthesis: when living systems are connected, they regulate. Regeneration works with that tendency, helping water, energy, and biodiversity rebalance together so human progress and planetary flourishing can advance in the same direction.

Mission
Make landscape impact on local climate legible so regeneration can scale with clarity, integrity, and measurable outcomes.
What we are building
A fast, interactive globe that layers satellite-derived Earth metrics into simple narratives: water cycle function, vegetation performance, thermal dynamics, and resilience.
Principles
Data provenance, matched baselines, clear uncertainty communication, and a bias toward usability.
