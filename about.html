About

Planetary Pulse is an Earth visualization and analytics platform focused on communicating ecosystem functional change at a glance.

Mission
Make ecological change legible so regeneration can scale with clarity, integrity, and measurable outcomes.
What we are building
A fast, interactive globe that layers satellite-derived Earth metrics into simple narratives: water cycle function, vegetation performance, thermal dynamics, and resilience.
Principles
Data provenance, matched baselines, clear uncertainty communication, and a bias toward usability.
