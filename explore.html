Explore is the interactive heart of Planetary Pulse. It evolves into a metric-driven analysis view with time sliders, regional bookmarks, and narrative tours. The goal is to deliver the same Earth Metrics for any location so landscape impact on local climate can be compared consistently. For now, use the selector below to preview each metric category with a clear visual tint.

The intent is simple: compare how different landscapes regulate heat, water, and vegetation function over time. Climate is the macro-pattern that emerges from these local conditions.
