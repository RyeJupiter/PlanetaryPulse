Project Atlas

We have compiled a list of regeneration projects across the globe. The same Earth Metrics data available for any location selected on the Explore page is available for these projects, enabling comparisons and documentation of impact using traceable, publicly available datasets so landscape impact on local climate is visible and comparable. Want your project included? Use the Contact page to share a lead.

The goal is not to claim regeneration by narrative alone, but to observe whether landscapes are becoming more capable of cooling, hydrating, and buffering extremes over time.
