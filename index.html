Planetary Pulse

Planetary Pulse is a metric-first Earth visualization platform that makes landscape impact on local climate visible and comparable. It combines a live global globe with measurable Earth metrics (hydrology, vegetation function, thermal regulation, albedo, aerosols, and resilience) so regeneration projects, researchers, and decision-makers can understand where change is happening and why. The goal is a clear, credible, and repeatable way to see ecological outcomes across regions and time.

Climate is not only atmospheric. It emerges from landscapes: how water moves, how vegetation behaves, and how energy is absorbed or released. Planetary Pulse is built to make those land-driven dynamics visible at scale.
