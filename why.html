Why Planetary Pulse

Regeneration is happening, yet verification remains fragmented. Planetary Pulse makes landscape impact on local climate visible so improvements can be tracked consistently.

The problem
Ecological outcomes are often communicated through isolated metrics, inconsistent baselines, and hard-to-interpret reports.
The opportunity
Satellite and climate datasets can reveal landscape impact on local climate in hydrology, vegetation, thermal regulation, and resilience when aggregated into clear Earth metrics.
Our approach
Planetary Pulse will compare project areas to matched controls over time, integrate multiple Earth metrics, and clearly present uncertainty and resolution limits.
The outcome
A shared, credible language of change that helps practitioners, funders, communities, and policymakers see what is working and where to focus next.

Climate is not only atmospheric. It is the macro-pattern that emerges from countless local land conditions repeated across space and time. Restoring landscape function is therefore a practical path to stabilizing climate behavior.
